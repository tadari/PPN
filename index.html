About Us

Here at The People's Pollution Network, we realize that there is a growing but invisible problem across the globe. Air pollution is a topic that is brought up frequently, but do you ever truly know how it effects you personally?

More often than not, people don't know what they're breathing other than the naturally present gases in air. For this reason, we're creating a small, lightweight, but advanced device that can give you all of that info and more!

The Answer

Using the latest and greatest air sensory technology, we're creating an easily portable multi-function tool and app combo. Know what's in the air around you, where ever and whenever you go!

Take one of our sensors with you on your daily commute, and get real-time level readings of the airborne CO2, ozone, and solid particulates, just to name a few. Keep one in your home and get phone alerts about hazardous changes in air makeup and quality, such as a dangerous spike in carbon monoxide.

The Math

As the amount of air pollution increases, so do the rates of lung related illnesses.

Scientific studies have shown that certain chemicals in the air are hazardous, but even more serious are particles that are 2.5µm or less in diameter. These particles are so small that the lungs have a hard time expunging them, and so lead to infections and/or other lung illnesses.

Our Mission

To help you get informed about what's in your air, and what you can do about it!

We want to help people learn, but we also hope that our tool is used to learn and then push for changes that will benefit themselves, those around them, and perhaps even the world!
